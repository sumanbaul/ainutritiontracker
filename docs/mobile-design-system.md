# NutriLens mobile design system

Cyber Dark uses Void Black `#070A12`, Midnight `#0D1220`, and Elevated `#141A2C` surfaces. Electric Indigo, Hyper Violet, Liquid Cyan, Aurora Blue, Plasma Pink, and Signal Green provide restrained functional accents. Cyber Light uses pale blue-white surfaces with the same indigo/cyan identity and reduced glow.

Cards use rounded 22px geometry, high-contrast text, and Material 3 semantics. Gradients are centralized in `AppGradients`; feature widgets do not define product gradients. Motion should remain brief and respect platform reduced-motion behavior. Touch targets remain at least 48 logical pixels and progress always includes textual values.
