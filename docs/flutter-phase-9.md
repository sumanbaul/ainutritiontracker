# Flutter Phase 9

Phase 9 turns the mobile foundation into a functional backend-connected product milestone. Startup verifies backend health and development identity, fetches the backend profile, and routes missing profiles to onboarding. Existing profiles enter the app shell.

Implemented mobile surfaces include onboarding/profile creation, profile protocol display, authoritative Today totals plus targets, confirmed meal history, weight entry/history, a deliberate Phase 10 capture preview, theme settings, pull-to-refresh, and readable empty/error states.

The current temporary identity remains `X-Development-User-Id`. Production authentication and camera/image analysis remain out of scope.
